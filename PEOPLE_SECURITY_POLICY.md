# 🛡️ PEOPLE SECURITY & ETHICAL USE POLICY
## Waqar School Management System (Secure Portal)

**Hadaayat (Instructions) for All Staff & Admins:**

### **1. 📱 Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)**
- **Rule:** Har Admin ko apne phone mein Google Authenticator install karna lazmi hai.
- **Why?** Agar password chori ho jaye, tab bhi portal khul nahi sakega bina aapke phone ke code ke.
- **Status:** **SUPER-ADMIN ke liye forced hai.** Dashboad khulne se pehle setup karna hoga.

### **2. 🚫 No Account Sharing**
- **Rule:** Ek login ID sirf ek insaan use karega. 
- **Warning:** Agar doosre laptop se login kiya gaya, to pehla wala automatic log-out ho jayega.
- **Why?** Accountability ke liye lazmi hai ke pata ho kisne kya change kiya.

### **3. 📸 No Screenshots of Passwords**
- **Rule:** Kabhi bhi password ka screenshot na lein aur na hi paper par likh kar desk pe rakhein.
- **Why?** 80% hacks tab hote hain jab koi "Password123" desk pe likha hua dekh leta hai.

### **4. 💬 No WhatsApp Credentials**
- **Rule:** WhatsApp ya Email par kabhi kisi ko login details ya OTP na bhejein.
- **Warning:** School management ka koi bhi banda aap se OTP nahi maangega.

### **5. 🔒 Device Hygiene**
- **Rule:** Hamesha kaam khatam hone par **"LOGOUT"** karein. Sirf browser band karne se account band nahi hota.
- **Warning:** Public Wi-Fi ya doosre ke system par login na karein.

---

## 📢 **IMPORTANT REMINDER**
*"80% hacks code se nahi, insaan ki ghalti se hotay hain."*

Aapki laparwahi pore school ka data khatre mein daal sakti hai. Hamesha Zero-Trust policy apnayein: **Kisi par yaqeen na karein, hamesha verify karein.**
